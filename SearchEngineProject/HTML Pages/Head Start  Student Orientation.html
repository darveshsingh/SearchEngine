What is Head Start?

We want to ensure you have a smooth transition to the University of Windsor so we have planned a full day of campus tours, faculty presentations, and opportunities to make new friends! You also will have a chance at winning $1,500 towards your tuition. Have questions? Feel free to email us at eventregistration@uwindsor.ca.

 

FACULTY SPECIFIC DATES vs. GENERAL DATES

  • Faculty Specific Dates provide students with an opportunity to meet professors, ask program specific questions, and tour the buildings where they will have regular classes. 

  • Faculty Specific Dates are not mandatory to attend, and you will still receive all necessary information at a General Head Start date
