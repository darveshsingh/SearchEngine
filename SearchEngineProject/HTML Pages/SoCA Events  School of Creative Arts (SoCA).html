Concerts, Guest Artists, Exhibitions, Talks and Recitals

SoCA Gallery Exhibitions

SoCA Artist Talks

The  SoCA Artist Talks series feature professional visual and multmedia artists —faculty and guests— at the new SoCA Armouries' in either the Performance Hall or SoCA Gallery.  Free event.

SoCA Music Guest Artists

The  SoCA Music Guest Artists series feature professional musicians—faculty and guests—in concert, at the SoCA Armouries' new Performance Hall.  Tickets $20 Adults and Seniors; Students $10.

SoCA Music Student Concerts 

Student Ensemble Concert series feature performances by our student ensembles. Featured University of Windsor ensembles include: UWindsor Chamber Choir, Jazz Combo, Jazz Ensemble, University Singers, Wind Ensemble and Percussion Ensemble, and speical showcase concerts. Tickets $20 Adults and Seniors; Students $10.  

Student Third Year and Graduation Recitals Third year and graduating Music students will perform solo recitals in the Armouries Performance Hall or at a variety of venues around Windsor.  

Community Concert series at SoCA Armouries. Sundays at 2 pm these free recitals feature performances by young musicians in the studios of ORMTA teachers and other music teachers in our community.

Purchase your tickets to any of our concerts in advance online at our secure website or buy at the door. Our goal is to inform and connect music lovers, whether they are students, alumni, or our friends in the community.
