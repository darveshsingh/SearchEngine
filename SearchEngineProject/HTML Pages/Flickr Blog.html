Flickr Pro Lou Noble is a Los Angeles-born Hollywood set medic and photographer who specializes in portrait photography. As Editor-in-Chief of The Photographic Journal, Lou credits Flickr with nurturing his passion for photography early on in his career by connecting with our incredible community of creatives.
