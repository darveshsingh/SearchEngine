LIBRO-EPIC Social Enterprise Workshop Series


As part of the Libro EPIC Social Impact Initiative, the Libro EPIC Social Enterprise Workshop Series aims to encourage and support social innovation in the Windsor-Essex community by offering free workshops focused on social innovation and social enterprise. Participants will learn the basics of social enterprise and how to build and start a social enterprise.  

 

Please check our event page for upcoming workshops. Registration is free! 

Libro EPIC Workshop Series 2019

2. Business for Good: Social Enterprise 101– July 23, 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM
3. Start a Social Enterprise: Make an Impact – August 22nd, 5:00 PM – 7:30 PM

Libro EPIC Social Enterprise Competition (September 19th, 2019)

Pitch for a chance to participate in the Libro EPIC Social Enterprise Program to further develop your social enterprise idea through mentorship, programming and up to $8,000 in funding. Details of the competition here.

Libro EPIC Social Enterprise Program (October 2019 to February 2020)

The Libro EPIC Social Enterprise Program is a 16-week accelerator program where participants will receive mentorship, programming and up to $8,000 in funding.
