Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences @ UWindsor

UWindsor’s Faculty of Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences (FAHSS) offers more degree options and combinations than most other Canadian universities. Here, you’ll learn the right skills to succeed — critical thinking, communication and problem solving. Your degree can take you in any direction. We’ll give you the support you need to follow your passion.

  • Combine your interests in a double major specific to your career goals
  • Build your knowledge and diversify your employment skills by choosing courses in a variety of subjects
  • Prepare for professional programs such as Law and Education
  • Add a certificate to your degree

Prepare yourself for leadership roles in your future career through special courses such as Mentorship and the Bystander Initiative.

Visit Dillon Hall

Magnificent Dillon Hall, built in 1928, is a classic architectural building - yet its classrooms are equipped with modern technology. Located on its lower level is the Student Leadership and Success Centre. It offers support for our students to achieve their academic goals through personalized services and helpful workshops on such topics as making the transition to university, how to study smarter and developing leadership skills.
