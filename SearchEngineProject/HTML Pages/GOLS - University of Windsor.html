Our Symposium

The Georgie-Odette Leadership Symposium is an annual event targeted at students and business professionals bringing together proven business leaders who speak on various aspects of successful leadership.

It is funded by the Richard Peddie Leadership (RPLI) initiative in order to help develop students’ leadership capabilities at the Odette School of Business, University of Windsor.
