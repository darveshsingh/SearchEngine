SoCA Music - Buy Tickets

** Please note: All online ticket purchases are final. No refunds. Tickets may be exchanged for another concert up to 48 hours before a concert. Tickets have no cash value after the concert has taken place.

YOUR TICKETS WILL BE WAITING FOR YOU AT THE CONCERT'S TICKET TABLE.

Also note:  This secure e-commerce software will not send you a confirmation email. Please, after checking out, print the receipt page for your records.

If you have problems completing your order, please email tpittman@uwindsor.ca for assistance.

All required fields are marked with an asterisk *.
