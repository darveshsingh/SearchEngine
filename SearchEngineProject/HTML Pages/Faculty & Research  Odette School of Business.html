Faculty & Research

At Odette, we aim to be among the most productive and progressive business schools in Canada, measured by the publication of high-impact research articles in leading academic journals. As the state of business constantly evolves, our goal is to continually be at the forefront of analysis and understanding with relevant research studies that benefit students, business scholars, and the business community.

We are a methodologically diverse school that promotes discipline-based and interdisciplinary research grounded in theory, focused on both practice-driven intellectual contributions and business education. In particular, our faculty has committed its research endeavors to the following six fundamental issues:

  • Leadership and Governance
  • Transformation, Innovation and Organizational Performance
  • The Economy of the Future
  • International Business
  • Business Education
  • Corporate Social Responsibility

We invite you to learn more about the interesting and engaging research activities taking place at Odette.
