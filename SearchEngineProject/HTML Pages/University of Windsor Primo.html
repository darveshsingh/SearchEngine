General Search tips

  • Search terms are connected by AND unless OR or NOT are specified between the words and phrases.
  • Phrase Search: Add quotation marks to ensure your words appear together in any items in your search results. E.g. "global warming"
  • Wild Card: Enter a question mark to perform a single character wildcard search. For example, wom?n looks for women and woman
  • Truncation: Enter an asterisk to perform a truncated search. For example, cultur* looks for culture, cultures, cultural, etc.
