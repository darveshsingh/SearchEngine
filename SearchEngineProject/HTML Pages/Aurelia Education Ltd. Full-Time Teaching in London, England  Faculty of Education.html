Aurelia Education Ltd.: Full-Time Teaching in London, England

FULL-TIME TEACHING IN LONDON, ENGLAND- PRIMARY AND SECONDARY AVAILABLE FOR SEPTEMBER 2019

Enjoy full time teaching in your own classroom!

Aurelia Education Ltd, a Canadian Teaching agency that supports teachers in finding full time roles in England, is looking for primary and secondary teachers to join them this September.

If interested, please contact Aurelia directly at aurelia@aureliaeducation.com or call 613-898-8909. 

VACANCY SUMMARY: 

  • English literature and Primary roles at an International Baccalaureate school
  • Special Education Teacher
  • Physical Education teacher 
  • Full time supply at one specific high school (guaranteed 5 days a week)
  • Maths, Science and English roles available
  • French speaking cover teacher at an IB school

PRIMARY VACANCIES: 

Roles: Primary Teacher for International Baccalaureate school 
Location: Wembley, London
School description: 

  • Primarily french speaking school with 27 languages spoken by staff members and students
  • 40% of lessons are taught in English and 60% in french
  • Strong use of technology and modern teaching styles
  • Focus on "whole learning" of the student including professionalism, respect, critical thinking and life goals

Roles: Year 7 Special Education Teacher
Location: Ickenham, London
School description:  

  • Special Needs school in North West, London
  • Ages 5-18, with class sizes of no larger than 9
  • 3 Canadians on staff, all who will be going into their second and third years with the school
  • Special Needs experience not a requirement as the school is more so looking for someone with a willingness to learn and the right attitude

Roles: Reception, Year 3, 6  and full time internal supply POSITIONS
Location: Wembley, London (two schools)
School description:

  • Two schools working together with a third school rated Good by OFSTED
  • Year 3 and Year 6 positions will begin as part time teaching, part time cover to allow for additional support and training to be put in place before going full-time in the second term
  • Approximately 10 newly qualified overseas teachers beginning in September
  • Currently three Canadian teachers on staff, all going into their second or third year with the schools

Roles: PE/Health teacher
Location: Wembley, London
School description:

  • #1 school in London based on progress and performance of the students
  •  Rated Outstanding by OFSTED
  • Looking for a PE/Health teacher to teacher across the year groups (5 to 11 year olds)
  • 10 Canadians teachers will be on staff September 2019
  • Fantastic training and support in place as they have employed overseas teachers with us for over 5 years

HIGHSCHOOL VACANCIES: 

Roles: English Literature Teacher 
Location: Wembley, London
School description: 

  • International Baccalaureate school 
  • Primarily french speaking school with 27 languages spoken by staff members and students (French is NOT a requirement)
  • 40% of lessons are taught in English and 60% in french
  • Strong use of technology and modern teaching styles
  • Focus on "whole learning" of the student including professionalism, respect, critical thinking and life goals

Roles: Cover and English teachers
Location: Wembley, London 
School description: 

  • Duchess Katherine Middleton visited campus earlier this school year to discuss their support put in place for mental health of students
  • Head of Art was voted “World’s Best Teacher of 2018”
  • Training program for new teachers created by an Aurelia Canadian teacher who has been with the school for 4 years now
  • Approximately 10 Canadians on staff for September 2019
  • Cover role is supplying full-time at the school, guaranteed 5 days a week

Roles: Keystage 5 Special Education Teacher
Location: Ickenham, London
School description: 

  • Special Needs school in North West London
  • Classroom of six 16/17 year old students with Severe Learning Difficulties focused on life skills learning
  • School is highly equipped for all support needs including physical therapies and life skill set ups such as kitchen facilities
  • Special Needs experience not a requirement as the school is more so looking for someone with a willingness to learn and the right attitude
  • Will begin in a training role until the end of the first half term and will then take on the class full time before the Christmas break

Roles: PE/Health teacher for primary school 
Location: Wembley, London
School description:

  • #1 school in London based on progress and performance of the students
  • Rated Outstanding by OFSTED
  • Looking for a PE/Health teacher to teacher across the year groups (5 to 11 year olds)
  • 10 Canadians teachers will be on staff September 2019
  • Fantastic training and support in place as they have employed overseas teachers with us for over 5 years
  • Secondary training will be accepted if the candidate has had experience working/volunteering with primary ages in a similar field
