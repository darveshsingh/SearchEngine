Economics Help Centre

Economics Help Centre

Resources:             

  • Staffed by graduate and teaching assistants to assist students in understanding course material

(Some are assigned to support students in specific courses, while others are available to support all students)

 

  • Although, some computers are available for student use with a TA/GA, including use of the program MYECONLAB, you should bring your personal computer if available

 

Audience:

  • Open to students enrolled in an undergraduate economics course including non-majors

Location:

  • G139 Chrysler Hall North

 

Hours: 

 

(Please refer to your course syllabus, or contact your instructor, for hours of operation)
