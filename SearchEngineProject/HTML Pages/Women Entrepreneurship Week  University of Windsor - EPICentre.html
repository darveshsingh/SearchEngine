Women Entrepreneurship Week

October 30th, 2019 | Blog, Featured, News, Newsletter

More than 200 universities and colleges in 30 countries hosted Women Entrepreneurship Week on October 19-26, 2019. This week was comprised of panel discussions, workshops, seminars, and conferences – events of all kinds – to highlight trailblazing women entrepreneurs from all disciplines. Our focus at EPICentre was to highlight young women entrepreneurs that have participated in EPICentre programs and activities, as well as, recent graduates from the University of Windsor.

As we see the world start to shift towards a more inclusive economy, we strongly believe that promoting these activities are pivotal to help strengthen the landscape of women in business, especially women entrepreneurs. We’d like to thank our panellists for taking the time to participate in this discussion that helps to promote #movingthedial forward.

Women Entrepreneurs – Panellists:

Ola Ahmed
Recent graduate from the University of Windsor, Graduate Studies, Biotechnology. Founder of ? Kindness Café – a social enterprise that incorporates a “pay as you feel” concept from recused surplus and unsold food.

Taylor Lanoie
Recent graduate from the University of Windsor, Business Administration. Founder of Taylor Lanoie Social Media Content Creation – creating authentic, quality content for both global and local brands.

Hanlu Li
Recent graduate from the University of Windsor, Graduate Studies, Business Administration. Co-Founder of? Ani & Fabi – pre-measured high-quality baking kits along with an easy to follow recipe delivered to your door.

Moderator:

Sydney Thompson Recent graduate from the University of Windsor, Business Administration. EPICentre Program and Event Lead.
