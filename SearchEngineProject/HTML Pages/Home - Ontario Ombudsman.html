The Ontario Ombudsman promotes fairness, accountability and transparency in the public sector by investigating public complaints and systemic issues within his jurisdiction.
Learn More about the ontario ombudsman

  • Have a complaint? Go To Have A Complaint Page Start the process here

As of May 1, 2019, the Ontario Ombudsman can now take complaints about child protection services and French language services. The Ombudsman has created dedicated units of specialized staff to answer questions, address concerns, conduct investigations, and review complaints.
