Updates

Licensed Child Care Center

October 2019

NEWS FLASH...We are looking to hire a Child Care Centre Supervisor.  If you are interested please go to the contacts tab on our website and press the Child Care Centre related careers tab.  

WE ARE HIRING!  Great Beginnings is looking for Supply Staff to fill in for permanent Staff vacancies due to vacation/personal/sick leave and internal postings.  If you have your Early Childhood Education Diploma and are registered with the College of Early Childhood Educators of Ontario, please contact us today!  We would love to have you join our team!

If you need care for your children 6 weeks to 12 years, let us know!  We offer a great child care program here at our licensed child care centre.  We look forward to helping you meet your education and work goals by caring for your little ones.

And remember, we reserve 75% of our spaces for the students, staff and faculty of the University of Windsor.

Licensed Home Child Care Program

October 2019

If you need a flexible child care schedule then our Home Child Care Program is the program for you! Contact us today; we would love to help you out!  We have spaces available for all age groups.

Also, if you are interested in becoming a Home Child Care Provider, caring for children 6 weeks to 12 years in your home, contact our Home Child Care Coordinator/Visitor.  She will work diligently to make your Home Child Care Business a reality!

We look forward to meeting you!
