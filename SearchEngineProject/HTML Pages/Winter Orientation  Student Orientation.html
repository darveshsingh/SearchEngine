Winter Orientation

Winter Orientation is for students admitted for the spring semester and prepares students for their educational experience at the Univerity of Windsor, including an opportunity to chat with an academic advisor. Guests are encouraged to attend Winter Orientation with their student to learn about the University of Windsor student experience and how they can support their student. We look forward to welcoming you to the Lancer Family!

Register NOW!

International Student? We have orientation programs just for you:

  • Undergraduate, Graduate and MEng Students can find information on the International Student Centre's Website.
  • Students attending the Centre for English Language Development can find information about their orientation program here.

Parking Pass

Will you be driving to Winter Orientation? Download and print the attached parking pass for FREE parking on January 4th.

Students looking to purchase a parking pass will have to wait for the one day sale, the date is TBD. Those without a parking pass are still subject to getting a parking ticket on campus if they do not have a parking pass. You are encouraged to park in the pay-n-display lots or the garage during this time. 

Backup Date

The back-up date for winter orientation is Thursday, January 9th from 5 pm to 7:30 pm in Dillon Hall 267.

All registered participants will be notified via email in the event that Winter Orientation is rescheduled due to inclement weather.

Schedule

We have a full day of activities planned for you! Winter Orientation will begin at 10:00 AM. Attending this orientation will allow you the opportunity to become familiar with your new campus and learn about techniques, and services offered to help you succeed while at the University of Windsor.  

To view the full schedule, click here.
 
 

Presentations to be Covered 

If you aren't able to make it to the Winter Orientation presentations don't worry, the presentation slides will be posted here following orientation.
