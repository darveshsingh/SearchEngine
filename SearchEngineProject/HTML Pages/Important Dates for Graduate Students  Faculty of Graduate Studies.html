Important Dates for Graduate Students

Review also all important academic dates from the Office of the Registrar and the tuition refund deadlines (for students completing research requirement only)

Fall term 2019

September 2:     

Labour Day (statutory holiday)     

September 3:

Law classes begin  

September 5:    

Fall term classes begin             

September 16:

Last day to Drop Fall classes in Law

September 18:  

Academic Add/Drop Date: Last day for late registration and change of courses    

September 20:

Final oral defense deadline: last day for the final oral defense of dissertations, theses and major papers for Fall Convocation 2019; and

Format-checking deadline: last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office, for students who are planning to meet Phase I deadline for Fall term 2019.

September 27:

Fall convocation deadline: last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers in the Office of Graduate Studies for Fall Convocation 2019; and   

Phase I deadline: last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers under Phase I (100% tuition refund) of the  tuition fee refund policy for the Fall term 2019.

October 2:

Financial Drop Date for Fall semester

October 14:         

Thanksgiving Day (statutory holiday) (no classes, University closed).

October 12-20  

Reading Week

October 15:

Format checking deadline for Phase II: last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office, for students who are planning to meet the Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Fall term 2019.

October 18:

Phase II deadline: last day for completion of all requirements for graduate degrees under Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Fall term 2019.

October 19:

Fall Convocation.

November 13:

Last day to voluntarily withdraw from Fall term courses.

November 28:

Last day to send a doctoral dissertation to the External Examiner for a January 2020 defense.

December 15: 

Winter term fees are due and payable.

Deadlines for depositing theses, dissertations and major papers before the end of Fall term 2019:

  • Friday, December 13 at 4:30 p.m.: format checking deadline for Fall term 2019: last day to submit for format review dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office.  
  • Friday, December 20 at 4:30 p.m.: deposit deadline for Fall term 2019: last day to complete the final submission (deposit) of dissertations, theses and major papers in the Graduate Studies office for the Fall term 2019. Students who do not complete the final deposit by December 20th must register for the Winter term 2020 - refer to details about registration and tuition refund deadlines for Winter term 2020.

Dec. 24, 2019- Jan. 1, 2020    

University offices closed for Christmas recess.

Winter term 2020

January 6, 2020

Winter term classes begin

January 14, 2020

The earliest date a PhD defense can be scheduled in 2020.

January 17, 2020

Academic Add/Drop Date for Winter term: Last day for late registration and change of courses

January 20, 2020

Last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office for students who are planning to meet the Phase I deadline (100% tuition) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020.        

January 27, 2020    

Phase I deadline: Last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers under Phase I (100% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020.

February 2, 2020

Financial Drop date for Winter semester

February 10, 2020

Last day to submit for format checking dissertations, theses and major papers to the Graduate Studies office for students who are planning to meet the Phase II deadline (50% tuition) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020

February 14, 2020  

Phase II deadline: Last day to deposit dissertations, theses and major papers under Phase II (50% tuition refund) of the tuition fee refund policy for Winter term 2020

February 15-23, 2020

Reading Week

February 17, 2020

Family Day Holiday: University closed

February 21, 2020

University offices closed

March 1, 2020

Last day to file an application for Spring graduation

March 13

Last day to voluntarily withdraw from Winter term courses. After this date students remain registered in courses and receive final grades as appropriate.

March 20, 2020

Deadline for recommending a PhD external examiner for students who plan to graduate at the Spring 2020 Convocation

April 3

Last day of classes

April 10

Good Friday – University closed.
