The Office of Open Learning

Welcome.

We're glad you found us.

The Office of Open Learning is a rapidly growing, dynamic team, with a growing local and national reputation. Our office is charged with inspiring and leading on strategic, creative and innovative developments in online learning, open learning environments, distance education and open education across the University.

 

If you are interested in the possibilities of expanding your teaching through the use of pedagogically sound open, online or blended materials- we can help.

 

The Office of Open Learning welcomes opportunities to work in partnership with colleagues across the University, in all Faculties, in the Centre for Teaching and Learning, and in the Library, with support from Information Technology Services. We invite discussion with other approprate units to develop opportunities for students to engage in high quality open and online learning. The Office will work with you to promote good educational design for: flexibility, inclusivity and enhanced learner engagement.
