Scholarship at UWindsor Guides

Adding your paper to Scholarship at UWindsor

To add a work to Scholarship at UWindsor please follow the guide below. If you'd like to request help in uploading your works, please contact scholarship@uwindsor.ca and we'd be happy to asssist. Video Tutorials are also available on variety of topics related to Scholarship at UWindsor.

If you have any questions please contact Mita Williams (mita@uwindsor.ca).

Quick Guide

(1) Go to Scholarship at UWindsor and sign in with your UWIN ID and password
(2) Hit "Submit Research" under author corner and select the department you want to upload to.
(3) Fill out the submit form with as much information about your article as possible and upload the appropriate version of your article. (visit our publisher posting policies page)
(4) Submit.
(5) Your article will then be reviewed and posted. 

After submitting your materials a preview screen will appear. Read through all of the information carefully, checking for errors. You will also receive an email confirming your submission. This email will also give you a link to check the status of your paper and make revisions until it is publicly posted.

NOTE: Your work will not be publicly available until an administrator posts it after checking the submission. If you have any questions please contact scholarship@uwindsor.ca.
