With $201.4 billion in net assets as of June 30, 2019, the Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan is the largest single-profession pension plan in Canada.

An independent organization, it invests the pension fund's assets and administers the pensions of 327,000 active and retired teachers in Ontario.
