    Welcome to UWindsor Self-Service Client Portal

    The main objective of this portal is to empower faculty, staff and students to be able to ask questions, find answers, and find solutions to common issues in a time-efficient manner. This self-service portal also allows clients to electronically submit their service requests and report issues by opening tickets for each of the services listed in our Service Catalogue.
