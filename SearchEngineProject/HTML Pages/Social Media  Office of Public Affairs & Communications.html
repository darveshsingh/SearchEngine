Social Media

Social Media Services provides support to social media channel administrators in departments across campus.

Services offered include:

  • Training courses on maintenance of your social media outlet
  • Consultation for your online presence and messaging
  • Design of individual graphics and other digital support
  • Guidance for your social media channel

The goal is to help internal clients manage their social media channels that engage, share valuable information, build communities and encourage two-way dialogue.

Departments who do not have a social media presence can still participate in social media. Contact the Social Media Co-ordinator to learn how your messages can be shared with our networks of over 145,000 followers!

UWindsor main social media channels

  UWindsor's Facebook Channel   UWindsor's Twitter Channel   UWindsor Instagram Channel   UWindsor YouTube Channel   UWindsor LinkedIn Channel

All of the training, consultation, graphic design and video services of the Web Communications office are offered free of charge to the University community.

Secondary social media channels

These channels are run by individual UWindsor administrators—connect with our communities and join the conversation.

Please email Dylan Kristy, Social Media Coordinator, if you would like your channel added to this list or require that your link be updated.
