Welcome to the University of Windsor

 

Thank you for selecting the University of Windsor as your employer of choice!

As you begin a new chapter of your career at the University of Windsor, I am pleased to welcome you to our Community. I am confident that you will find working at the University a very positive and rewarding experience. 

The University of Windsor has a history tracing back over one hundred and fifty years. Our Mission, “Enabling people to make a better world through education, scholarship, research and engagement” would not be achieved without dedicated faculty and staff who, directly or indirectly, support fifteen thousand students annually.

As a member of the University community, it is critical that you become familiar with our values:

  • Commitment to the highest intellectual standards
  • Commitment to the highest standards of human rights and freedoms
  • Commitment to have the student experience as a central consideration in decision making
  • Commitment to civility and respect for others
  • Commitment to applying teaching, research and creative activity to issues of importance to the world
  • Commitment to be a responsible steward of people, culture, resources and the environment

The Welcome to UWindsor Employee Orientation website has been designed to assist you in the transition to our organization. It will provide you with an overview of the University, its structure and the many services available to staff.

Once again, welcome to the University of Windsor, a great place to work, grow and learn!

Sincerely,

Rita LaCivita

Vice-President, Human Resources

New Employee Orientation - Requirements

As outlined in your Letter of Appointment, in order to complete the online New Employee Orientation requirements you will need to do the following within the first 2 weeks of employment:

Please note that you will need a UWin ID and password to access the training. Click here for details

  1. Become familiar with the information found on this website
  2. Complete all mandatory Health & Safety and Accessibility training (this link will connect you to all mandatory online training modules)
  3. Complete the Equity Survey
  4. Complete the "Welcome to UWindsor Employee Orientation Checklist"
