Book a Campus Tour

Let us show you why we’re Windsor Proud!

Campus tours are offered Monday to Friday at 10:30 am and 2:30 pm and are led by a current UWin Student Ambassador. We are ready to address your questions about student life and academics and show you around our beautiful campus.

The tour will include a general overview of the main campus and residences, as well as an opportunity to visit our downtown sites for the School of Creative Arts and the School of Social Work.

Please allow approximately 1.5 hours for your visit of main campus and please indicate when booking if you would like to visit the downtown campus.

Please wear comfortable footwear, and dress for the weather as we will proceed, rain or shine.

To book your campus visit, please telephone 1-800-864-2860 (toll free) or 519-973-7014 (local).

Future Engineering Student Shadow Days

This half day experience will run from 11:45 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., and includes being hosted by a current UWindsor undergraduate Engineering student, who will take you for a tour of the Engineering (CEI) building, followed by a meeting with the Assistant Dean, and sitting in on an Engineering Design class. You may also choose to attend an optional full campus tour offered by the Office of Student Recruitment after your shadowing experience.

November Shadow Days are now full, we will be announcing new days for January – March shortly.

Future Science Student Shadow Days

This half-day experience runs from 11:30am-2:00pm. It includes being hosted by a current UWindsor Science undergraduate student, who will take you for a short campus tour, lunch (sponsored by the Dean of Science) in the CAW Student Centre, and to sit in on a science undergraduate class. You may also choose to attend an optional full campus tour offered by the Office of Student Recruitment after your shadowing experience.

Register for Future Science Student Shadow Days

Future Nursing Student Shadow Days

This half-day experience will be offered by the Faculty of Nursing. You will be hosted by a current UWindsor Nursing undergraduate student and have the opportunity to sit in on a number of nursing undergraduate classes and labs, as well as meet with an Academic Advisor. You may also choose to attend an optional full campus tour offered by the Office of Student Recruitment after your shadowing experience.

Register for Future Nursing Student Shadow Days
