Description

SAFE LANCER is an essential tool to enhance your safety at University of Windsor. The app will send you important safety alerts and provide instant access to campus safety resources. SAFE LANCER is the official mobile safety app of the University of Windsor.

SAFE LANCER benefits include:

- Safety notifications: Receive instant notifications and instructions from campus safety when on-campus emergencies occur.

- Emergency help: contact campus safety staff quickly for help in an emergency.

- Campus safety resources: access all important University of Windsor safety resources in one convenient app.

Thousands of students, faculty, and staff at the University of Windsor are already using SAFE LANCER. Download today and ensure that you're prepared in the event of an emergency.
