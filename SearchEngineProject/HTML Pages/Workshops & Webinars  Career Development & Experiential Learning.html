Workshops & Webinars

 Career related workshops are free for all UWindsor students and alumni throughout each term. Log into mySuccess and look for the Career Events calendar to register ahead of time and reserve your spot! When space permits, walk-ins are welcome too.

Registration opens one week before the workshops. 

If spots are not available, you can sign up for the wait list.  If you are on the wait list and a spot becomes available, you will receive an email informing you that you have been automatically registered.

Unless otherwise specified, all workshops are located in the Joyce Entrepreneurship Center (JEC) 100 and will last up to 1 hour and 15 minutes.

What is Labour Market Information?

What is Labour Market Information? This webinar will teach you what labour market information is, why it is important, how to find it, and how to use it to give you an advantage in your career.

  • View the Webinar to learn more about the labour market.

 

If you would require a transcribed version of the audio for these online workshops, please contact us at careerservices@uwindsor.ca. 

Using LinkedIn to Build your Professional Network

Using LinkedIn to Build your Professional Network  This two part online workshop will introduce you to how to create a LinkedIn profile (Part 1) and how to use LinkedIn as a networking and job seeking tool (Part 2).

  • View the Web seminar for Part 1 to learn how to create a LinkedIn profile
  • View the Web seminar for Part 2 to learn how to network and use tools for job searching
