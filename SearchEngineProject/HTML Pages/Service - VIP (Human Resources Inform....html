    VIP (Human Resources Information System)

    Tags equity hris overtime-callin payroll pension position-control VIP casual-time-entry

    Human Resources Information System (HRIS) is a collection of related systems:

    VIP

    Our campus Human Resource / Payroll information system. 

    Equity (EQUITY)

    A variety of applications  for  Office of Human Right and Accessibility.  These include the Equity Census,  WEIMS,  and  DEAP.

    Position Control System (PCS)

    An application used by Finance, Budgets and Planning for position budget control

    UOW Overtime

    UOW Overtime call-in is the electronic over-time call-in tracking application designed to provide a common resource for over-time call-in tracking.

    Casual Time Entry

    An  application  developed for time entry and approval for student employees in Athletics

    Requesting Assistance

    Have a question about this service. Ask it here.

    To request assistance with any issues related to systems listed above, please click Open Ticket button on the right. This will bring up a form that you will need to fill-out and submit to open a support ticket with the I.T. Service Desk. You will receive a confirmation e-mail with the ticket reference number and additional instructions.
