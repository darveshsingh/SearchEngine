    THE PAYMENTS CANADA SUMMIT


    MAY 25 - 27, 2020

    Montréal, Québec




    #PayCanSUMMIT

    WHAT IS THE SUMMIT?

    Canada is undergoing a massive transformation in its payments ecosystem. The SUMMIT is the ideal meeting point for the payments community to exchange ideas on the future-state of the payments industry. Canada has a leading role to play in evolving the global payments ecosystem, and The SUMMIT is the place where we will see this transformation in action and the impact it will have on us as a nation, as an industry and as individuals.

    HIGHLIGHTS FROM 2019



    Disrupting an industry 
    from within


    INNOVATION

    Cadillac Fairview's EVP of Digital & Innovation, Jose Ribau discusses innovation in retail and the importance of merging digital with physical to maximize on the customer experience.







    Trust: The currency 
    of the digital future


    DATA

    If data is the new oil, then trust is the currency of the future. Interac's Chief Innovation Labs and New Ventures Officer, Debbie Gamble, discusses the exponential opportunities to share and exchange information, and also how misuse results in the erosion of trust.





    Technology and the future 
    of the retail customer experience


    ECONOMICS

    Walmart's Senior Director of International Payments & Financial Services, Mario de Armas, Independent Consultant (formerly Starbucks & Disney), Maria Smith, and The Home Depot Canada's Senior Director, e-Commerce & Advertising, Andrew Go are interviewed by Dilip Soman, Canada Research Chair in Behavioural Science and Economics at UofT about the future of retail.
