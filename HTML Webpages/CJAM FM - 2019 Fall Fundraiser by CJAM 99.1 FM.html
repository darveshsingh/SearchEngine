CJAM FM is a not-for-profit campus/community radio station broadcasting to Windsor Ontario and Metro Detroit. CJAM FM is the only campus community radio service in Windsor and we want to remain a vital voice in the area. We need funding to maintain basic operations and remain compliant to CRTC standards as a radio station.
