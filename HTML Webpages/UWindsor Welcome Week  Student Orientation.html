UWindsor Welcome Week

WHAT IS WELCOME WEEK?

Welcome Week is a period encompassing all the different events planned for incoming students. It is a completely student-run event supported by staff, organized by over 250 different coordinators ranging from the University of Windsor Student Alliance (UWSA), to Faculty Leaders proudly representing their respective programs, to various volunteer "Leaders" that ensure that incoming and returning students have the most entertaining and safe week as possible.

Why attend Welcome Week you ask? Free prize giveaways, new friendships, long-lasting memories and an opportunity to become acquainted with your faculty, staff, and leaders before even stepping into a classroom! Learn valuable lessons from seniors, locate all your classes, and take a positive first step towards your University career!
