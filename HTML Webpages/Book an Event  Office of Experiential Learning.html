Book an Event

Pop-up Station  

Make a statement on campus with a mobile Pop-up Station in a high-traffic area where you can interact with students in an open environment.

Coffee Chats  

Looking to connect with students in a specific faculty or area of study? Set up a Coffee Chat to meet-and-greet students in small groups.

Information Session  

Engage students with an Information and Q&A Session about your organization. Let us find you a great location on campus to suit all your presentation needs.

Hot Spot Bookings  

Promote your organization and interact with a wide variety of students by setting up a booth in the CAW Student Centre.

Job Fair  

Every January, the University of Windsor and St. Clair College partner together to host this recruitment event for our students and graduates. Sign up to be added to our mailing list.

Customize an Event  

There is no greater way to represent your organization than by developing an event the way you want it! Connect with our Events Team and develop something unique to attract career-ready talent on our campus today.

Hackathon / Technical Challenge 

Gain a better understanding of the student’s technical ability while promoting your brand.

Project-Based Learning 

Let us connect you with Faculty where you can collaborate on projects students can complete for your organization while benefiting to the real-world application of their classroom materials

Community Driven Research 

Let our students engage with what could drive change or impact their own communities in the way of community-driven research.

 

Let Us Come to You

Sometimes the best method of attracting talent to your organization is to bring that talent to you! Let our students see your daily operations, meet your team, and see for themselves why your business stands apart from the rest.
