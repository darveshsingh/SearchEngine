Hi Flickr!

Today we are very excited to start rolling out a redesign of several Groups pages, aka the “New Groups Experience”, to a small number of users. In particular, the redesign includes the Groups Overview page, Groups List page, Group Discussions page, and Group Photo Pool page. The rest of the Groups pages will still keep their original design for this initial release.

You made it all possible

This redesign was big and has been several months in the making, so we’d like to share some background of the project with you:

To understand how to improve Groups, we put out a survey that many of you took part in. We received thousands of long, detailed, and actionable responses from you. We also went back and read the feedback on the Help Forum from the last redesign of Groups in July 2013. Then, we stack-ranked this feedback to understand the biggest pain points you were experiencing with Groups.

Lastly, many more Flickr staff members became power groups users, joining, creating and becoming admins/mods of several groups to be able to reproduce your concerns and get more hands-on experience, particularly in the group administration and moderation department.

Collecting, reading, and understanding this massive amount of feedback from multiple sources was a challenging task, and we hope the steps we have taken with this release will address your largest pain points and show you the direction of things to come.

Depending on how the new groups experience performs, we’ll be rolling it out to more of you over the next couple of weeks. We will be closely monitoring your feedback as well as engagement and performance metrics, and are committed to hold off on a broad release until we are sure all critical bugs and performance issues are fixed.

Known bugs

There are also some existing bugs that we are aware of as we release to the initial group of users. We assure you we will be fixing these in the coming weeks.

* Groups List page is not working for members with > 1000 groups. We are working on a fix for this.
• Not all translations outside of English are complete
• Occasional missing loading indicator
• Cover photo position does not save at set position
• Photo credit is missing on the group cover photo Fixed!
• When leaving a group as the only admin, confirm warning dialog does not indicate so Fixed!
• Admin Blast needs to show a time stamp after adding
• Top Contributor: Displaying wrong call to action message when on filtered view by top contributor and photos from that contributor have been deleted by an admin. Fixed!
• Top contributor photo link on group overview page shows first photo added, not latest added Fixed!
• Support missing latest photo on top contributors Fixed!
• No informational message when there are no photos to select from on the group photo picker Fixed!
• Pending items does not include pending members Fixed!
• Selecting a top contributor does not change the drop-down to their name Fixed!
• Setting group photo back from private to public removes it from group pool
• Fave counter is blue on group pool display-item-tile
• Pending queue on Groups List page is inaccessible for groups with more than one million photos/posts

For those of you in the test bucket, you will be able to opt out (and/or opting back in) at any time during the beta by clicking the horizontal banner at the top of your screen.

One more thing

We are working on a new, amazing Notifications Center for Flickr. We heard you loud and clear, so the notifications center will also include new Groups-related notifications based on your feedback:

• A new discussion thread is started in a group you are a member of,
• Another member replied to a discussion thread you created / participated in
