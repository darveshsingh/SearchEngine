Applying to Graduate

All students must apply to graduate regardless of attendance at the Convocation Ceremony.

Typically, the deadlines to apply to graduate are:

March 1st for the Spring Convocation
August 1st for the Fall Convocation held in October

Typically, late applications to attend the ceremony, and the associated non-refundable fee, will be accepted from

March 2nd to one month prior to the Spring ceremony at $80
August 2nd to one month prior to the Fall ceremony at $80

To apply to graduate, please log on to UWinsite Student.

No applications will be accepted within the four weeks prior to the start of Convocation. The last date applications will be accepted for the Spring 2019 Convocation is Wednesday, May 1, 2019.
