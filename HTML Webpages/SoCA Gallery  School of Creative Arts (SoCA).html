SoCA Gallery

Located in the downtown core of Windsor, the SoCA Gallery is a not-for-profit university exhibition space which is used to support our current educational programs in the School of Creative Arts (SoCA). Its primary focus is to develop best practices in exhibition standards for our undergraduate and graduate students. We also aim to support, where resources permit*, the needs of the academic community more broadly as well as the work of alumni and the arts community more generally.

SoCA Gallery gives precedence to the following:

• MFA/BFA Program Exhibitions
• Faculty-directed/curated research exhibitions
• Course based programming
• Student organized group exhibitions
• Visiting artists exhibitions/workshops
• Community partnerships
• Alumni projects

*Gallery fees are not paid unless granted through external funding sources. 

For individuals or groups not affiliated with SoCA, a rental fee of $500/week will apply.

Submissions: Your application will be reviewed by the SoCA Gallery Committee.  The committee will decide if your submission is successful.  To apply, please go to: Online application to exhibit in the SoCA Gallery.  

Gallery handbook: /soca/sites/uwindsor.ca.soca/files/gallery_handbook_201920.pdf
