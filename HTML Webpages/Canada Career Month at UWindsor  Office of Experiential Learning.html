Canada Career Month at UWindsor

November marks the start of Canada Career Month! Join the University of Windsor's Career Development & Experiential Learning department as we shine a spotlight on how we help you access meaningful work and connect with your preferred future. 

Participate and Win!

Help us celebrate Canada Career Month and you could win one of two $50 giftcards from the campus bookstore. Two ways to win:

  1.  Attend one of our November career workshops.

    • Details below.

  2. Drop in to meet with one of our peer advisors.

    • Peer advising drop-in hours are Monday to Friday from 10am-4pm in JEC 100

?Any UWindsor student that does one of the above will be automatically entrered into a random draw. Two winners will be selected and contacted at their UWindsor email address in early December. Any questions can be directed to careerservices@uwindsor.ca.

What is Canada Career Month?

Each November, Canadians from coast-to-coast-to-coast celebrate the importance of accessing meaningful work and all those that help us in connecting with our preferred futures. Canada Career Month works to advance the agenda of improving access to career services and education so that all Canadians are better prepared to develop their careers and transition into the emergent labour market. Find out more at www.careermonth.ca.
