Opening Up Copyright Instructional Modules

These Opening Up Copyright Instructional Modules are open educational resources.

For an overview of the full set of modules that are envisioned, or to leave feedback, comments or suggestions regarding any proposed modules, please visit our Community Page.

The module series is funded through the University of Alberta’s Centre for Teaching and Learning Teaching and Learning Enhancement Fund.

This instructional module is not intended as legal advice.  All OUC modules are made available under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC-BY-4.0) International license.
